#NamePlaybackAudio

Click a name to edit (the pedal's display fits 12 ASCII chars; Enter to save). Click the playback badge to toggle One Shot. Upload a 44.1 kHz stereo WAV by dragging it onto a slot row, or click ⬆ / the empty-row label to choose a file. ⬇ downloads a slot's audio to your computer, ✕ clears the slot (the wav goes to the local trash folder first). When you're done: eject the volume, then reboot the pedal.
